A lifelong passion

Since my father taught me Excel when I was only 8, I developed a lifelong passion for numbers.

Everything I loved, all my favourite video games, I put to the spreadsheet.

Whatever my childhood interests - Physics, Biology, History - I always focused on the numbers.

Now, I put my passion to work as a Data Analyst.

Overcoming my failures

As a boy, I always wanted to program. But I bit off more than I could chew, jumped right into the deep end.

All of my efforts were met with compiler errors. All of my ambitions were frustrated.

Through study I changed that. Started from the fundamentals and overcame my frustrating failures.
